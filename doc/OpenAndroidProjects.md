## Developer verification impact

Android developer verification begins regional enforcement on September 30,
2026 for Brazil, Indonesia, Singapore, and Thailand, with global expansion in
2027 and beyond. The requirement applies to certified Android devices and can
affect third-party app stores and direct APK installs when an app is not
registered by a verified developer.

Open Android projects remain important because device certification, bootloader
unlockability, ADB access, and alternative OS support determine how much control
users keep over app installation and recovery paths. When evaluating a device or
ROM, check whether it preserves bootloader unlock, ADB sideloading, third-party
store installation, and documented rollback or recovery options.

References: [Android developer verification](https://developer.android.com/developer-verification),
[Android Developers Blog](https://android-developers.googleblog.com/2026/06/android-developer-verification.html),
